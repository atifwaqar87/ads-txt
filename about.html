About Us

We are a group of Pakistanis living abroad who built PakStream to help everyone feel a little closer to home. Whether you're craving the sound of local radio, TV shows, or the latest from Pakistani YouTubers, PakStream brings that taste of Pakistan to you — no matter where you are in the world.
