Pakistani YouTube Content

Discover Pakistani journalists, vloggers and content creators. You can embed videos directly from YouTube using the <iframe> tag. Follow the steps below:

  1. Find a YouTube video you want to share. Note its video ID (the part after v= in the URL).
  2. Add an <iframe> to your HTML with the video URL. For example:
<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
