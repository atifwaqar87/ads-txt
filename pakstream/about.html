About PakStream

PakStream is a community‑driven platform that aggregates links to Pakistani radio stations, TV channels, YouTube content, tweets and, in the future, rare goods. Our mission is to celebrate Pakistani culture and media by making it easy for diaspora and locals alike to discover and enjoy authentic content.

Because PakStream is hosted entirely on GitHub Pages, there is no backend server. We do not host the media ourselves; instead, we link to official sites and embed publicly available streams in compliance with their terms.

To display advertisements, we plan to use Google AdSense. Google’s policies require us to offer unique, original content and maintain clear navigation while avoiding scraped or copyrighted material【902347014358082†L34-L104】. We will ensure that our site meets these requirements before submitting it for approval.

PakStream is developed with respect for intellectual property and aims to provide a valuable resource for the community. If you have suggestions or contributions, please contact us.
